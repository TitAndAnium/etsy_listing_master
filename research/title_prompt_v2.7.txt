Do **NOT** output extra text, markdown, or comments.

## TITLE RULES (STRICT – ZERO TOLERANCE)
- ASCII-only ; ≤ 140 characters
- No emojis, prices, currency symbols, %, “% off”, ★, ✨, ♥
- [FOCUS_KEYWORD] must appear within first 5 words (unless force_keyword_logic=false)
- Max **one** ALL-CAPS word (allowed only if emotionally justified)
- No duplicate keywords or synonym-stacking (e.g. shirt/tee/t-shirt)
- Forbidden words: “best ever”, “cheap”, “free”, “guaranteed”

## STRUCTURAL STRATEGY (REQUIRED)
1. Anchor with FOCUS_KEYWORD + PRODUCT_TYPE
2. Blend tone (TONE_STYLE + STYLE_TREND)
3. Add visual modifiers (MOCKUP_STYLE, MOCKUP_MOOD, MOCKUP_COLOR)

## BUYER-RECEIVER STRATEGY
- BUYER → action-oriented phrasing “Surprise your sister with…”
- RECEIVER → identity/emotion “Perfect for proud dog moms”

## GIFT MODE
If GIFT_MODE=true: include gifting phrase in first 8 words, add occasion/relationship.  
If GIFT_EMOTION in [surprise, belonging, romantic] → one emotional ALL-CAPS word allowed.

## SEASONAL & AUDIENCE
Inject SEASONAL_CONTEXT (“spring wedding”, “holiday gift”) when present.  
Adapt diction to AUDIENCE_PROFILE (Gen Z playful, Millennial aesthetic, etc.).

## KEYWORD PRIORITY CHAIN
1 FOCUS_KEYWORD → 2 (GIFT_MODE+BUYER_VS_RECEIVER) → 3 PRODUCT_TYPE → 4 GIFT_EMOTION → 5 STYLE_TREND → 6 SEASONAL_CONTEXT → 7 MOCKUP_* + AUDIENCE_PROFILE
